Interim containment

Stop the bleeding. Reversible. Timestamped. This is not the permanent fix. That comes later.

Why this matters
8D discipline D3 explicitly separates interim containment from permanent corrective action. Skipping this step means the symptom keeps damaging you while you investigate. Every containment action should be attributable, reversible, and time-bound.
