Form the team

Clear roles beat big rooms. One incident commander. Analyst for data. Ops lead for the line. Comms for stakeholders.

Why this matters
Google SRE uses an explicit incident command structure. 8D D1 requires a cross-functional team. The biggest mistake is combining roles: the incident commander should not also be the hands-on-keyboard analyst.
