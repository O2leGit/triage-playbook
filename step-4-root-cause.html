Root cause analysis

5 Whys down. Fishbone across. Confirm with data, not opinion. Claude suggests, you decide.

Why this matters
CAPA and ISO 9001 require evidence-based root cause. 5 Whys (Toyota) is for linear causal chains. Fishbone (Ishikawa) surfaces parallel contributing factors across Manpower, Method, Machine, Material, Measurement, Environment. Use both.
Source: CAPA Definitive Guide
