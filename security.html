What the app does

Triage Playbook walks an operator through a 10-step structured investigation of any operational problem. It fuses incident management (SRE, ITIL), root cause analysis (A3, 5 Whys, Fishbone), and corrective and preventive action (8D, CAPA) into one workflow.

Who it is for

  • Operations leaders responding to scrap, downtime, quality escapes, service outages, deviations
  • Quality Assurance investigating CAPAs and non-conformances
  • SRE and IT running incident response and postmortems
  • Supply chain managing supplier issues, lot recalls, logistics deviations

What it is not

  • Not a replacement for a validated QMS in a regulated environment until ENTERPRISE tier plus validation pack is complete
  • Not a record of truth for GMP data. Links to your MES or ERP; never replaces them
  • Not an on-call or alerting platform. No paging, no on-call rotations, no sensor telemetry ingest
  • Not a replacement for TrackWise, Veeva Vault QMS, or MasterControl in regulated rollouts

Deployment tiers

Pick the tier that matches your compliance posture. Change tier at any time from the topnav pill.

DEV

Solo developer, prototype, training

Data lives in the browser via IndexedDB. No sign-in. No audit chain. No validation. Fastest iteration.

  • Storage: IndexedDB (local only). No server. No cloud sync.
  • AI: optional. Calls Anthropic Claude via Netlify Function. No PHI or GMP data should be entered in this tier.
  • Audit: best-effort log in IndexedDB. Not tamper-evident.
  • Use for: product evaluation, internal training, dogfooding. Not for regulated data.
TEAM

Small team, pilot, non-regulated production

Adds Part 11-style e-signature, hash-chained audit trail, enforced data classification, change-control gate, AI redaction on Restricted and GMP fields.

  • Storage: still local IndexedDB with optional Supabase sync layer (customer-hosted Postgres acceptable).
  • Authentication: magic-link email or OAuth 2.0 via Supabase Auth.
  • Audit: SHA-256 hash-chained. Tamper-evident. Exportable.
  • E-signature: user ID plus session reason plus record-hash bind. Meets Part 11 intent; not validated.
  • AI: data classification gate. Restricted and GMP fields are redacted before prompts leave the browser.
  • Change control: corrective actions cannot move past draft without a linked Change Control record ID.
  • Use for: pilot with a small team, non-regulated operational triage, pre-production validation.
ENTERPRISE

Regulated rollout, multi-site, GMP-ready

All TEAM features plus enterprise SSO, ERP lot lookup, Microsoft Graph outbound (no external webhooks), validation pack.

  • Authentication: Microsoft Entra ID (formerly Azure AD) SSO with conditional access.
  • Access control: group-based, mapped to quality units, sites, and product lines.
  • Integrations: SAP, Oracle, Veeva Vault, TrackWise via customer-hosted connectors. Lot or serial lookups validate against live systems.
  • Outbound messaging: Microsoft Graph only. No third-party webhooks unless on approved vendor list.
  • AI: DLP-scanned prompts and responses, audit-logged 10 years, configurable data boundary (e.g. Azure OpenAI in tenant).
  • Validation: GAMP 5 Category 4 pack (URS, FS, DS, IQ, OQ, PQ, risk, traceability). Executed with the customer's Quality Unit.
  • Use for: biotech, pharma, medical device, or any environment under 21 CFR Part 11, EU Annex 11, ISO 13485, GxP.

Data handling

What leaves your browser

  • Nothing, in DEV tier. No sync, no analytics, no tracking. The app even works offline.
  • AI prompts go to Anthropic Claude via a Netlify Function. Fields classified as Restricted or GMP are redacted before the prompt is built. AI can be disabled entirely in settings.
  • Feedback form sends what you type to chris@cotoole.com via Resend. You control the message.
  • Share links encode the playbook as a compressed blob in the URL. Only people you send the link to see it.

Sub-processors (TEAM and ENTERPRISE)

  • Netlify: static site host + serverless functions. SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA BAA on request.
  • Supabase: Postgres, Auth, Storage. SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA BAA on request, self-hostable for ENTERPRISE.
  • Anthropic: Claude API. SOC 2 Type II. Enterprise plan offers data-retention controls and zero-retention mode. ENTERPRISE customers can route via Azure OpenAI in-tenant instead.
  • Resend: transactional email. Used for feedback and scheduled digests. Optional; can be disabled.

Corporate IT FAQ

Does this touch our network?

In DEV, no. In TEAM and ENTERPRISE, traffic flows over standard HTTPS to the tenant you approve. Firewall rules and DLP apply as normal. No VPN or special ports required.

Can we self-host?

ENTERPRISE tier supports self-hosted Supabase (Postgres), self-hosted Netlify Edge alternatives (e.g. Cloudflare Workers in your tenant), and Azure OpenAI in your subscription for AI. The codebase is static HTML plus vanilla JS plus a handful of Node serverless functions. No hidden dependencies.

Can we turn AI off?

Yes. A single config flag disables all AI features. The 10-step workflow works fully without AI. You lose 5 Whys suggestions, Fishbone proposals, and auto-report generation. Everything else persists.

How do we decommission?

In DEV, clear IndexedDB in the browser. In TEAM/ENTERPRISE, we ship a one-shot export script (Postgres dump + Storage bucket tarball + audit log tarball). You own the data, we don't hold residuals.

What's the patch / release cadence?

DEV and TEAM: continuous. Every git push auto-deploys. ENTERPRISE: validated releases on a customer-agreed schedule (typically quarterly). Each release comes with regression test evidence, change log, and risk re-assessment.

Known limitations (honest list)

  • No multi-user real-time collaboration in DEV (by design). TEAM adds it via Supabase Realtime.
  • No native mobile app. PWA only. Install to home screen works on iOS and Android.
  • No on-call paging, alerting, or sensor telemetry. Integrate with your existing tools.
  • Current validation pack is template-only. Full GAMP 5 execution happens per-customer in ENTERPRISE.
  • No formal FedRAMP, ISO 27001, or HIPAA self-attestation today. Relies on sub-processor attestations.
  • AI model pinning and performance monitoring are ENTERPRISE-only.
