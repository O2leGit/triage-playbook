The method

Ten steps. Seven world-class standards.

Synthesized from 8D (Ford), A3 (Toyota), DMAIC (Six Sigma), Google SRE, ITIL 4, CAPA (FDA 21 CFR 820.100 / ISO 9001), and PDCA (Deming). Industry-agnostic by design.

The 10-step framework

Every step maps to at least two world-class standards. Short-term triage lives in steps 1-3. Long-term stability lives in steps 5-10. The loop-back at step 10 is what separates good teams from world-class ones.

Phase A - Triage (0-24 hours)

1. Define the problem

Severity, impact, 5W2H, is/is-not. A problem well-stated is half-solved.

8D D0-D2, SRE severity triage, A3 background

Phase A - Triage

2. Form the team

Incident commander, analyst, ops lead, quality, comms, stakeholders. Clear roles beat big rooms.

8D D1, SRE incident command

Phase A - Triage

3. Interim containment

Stop the bleeding. Reversible. Timestamped. Explicitly not a permanent fix.

8D D3, ITIL incident management

Phase B - Root cause

4. Data-driven root cause

5 Whys chain plus Fishbone (manpower, method, machine, material, measurement, environment). Confirm with data, not opinion.

A3 RCA, 8D D4, CAPA

Phase C - Long-term action plan

5. Permanent corrective actions

One action per confirmed root cause. Owner, due date, reversibility flagged.

8D D5/D6, A3 countermeasures

Phase C - Long-term action plan

6. Preventive actions (escape-point fix)

Why did our system let this leak? Preventive actions fix the hole in detection, not the original fault.

8D D7, CAPA preventive, ITIL problem management

Phase D - Governance

7. Governance, RACI, and blameless postmortem

Ownership, escalation rules, review cadence, postmortem template, and standardize the new way.

SRE postmortem culture, CAPA governance

Phase E - Monitoring

8. Metrics, thresholds, and response plan

Leading and lagging indicators. Red, yellow, green thresholds. Response plan for out-of-control signals.

DMAIC Control, SPC, lead/lag indicators

Phase F - Effectiveness

9. 30/60/90 day verification

Close the CAPA only with evidence. Pre-defined success criteria. Electronic signature on sign-off.

CAPA closure, PDCA Check

Phase G - Loop-back

10. Re-triage if metrics do not move

Ask three questions: was the root cause wrong, was the countermeasure inadequate, or did execution fail? Re-enter at the weakest link.

PDCA Act, A3 iteration
